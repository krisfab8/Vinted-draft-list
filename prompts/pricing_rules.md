# Pricing Principles
- Minimum target profit: £30 (sell price - buy price - Vinted fees)
- Prefer quick sell-through over vanity pricing
- Use stronger pricing only for clearly desirable brands/models
- Vinted takes ~5% + €0.70 per transaction — factor this in

# Suggested Pricing Formula
list_price = buy_price + target_profit + estimated_vinted_fee
e.g. bought for £15, target profit £30 → list at ~£50

# Markdown Schedule
- Day 7:  reduce by 10% from original list price
- Day 14: reduce by 15% from original list price
- Day 21: reduce by 20% from original list price

# Exceptions
- Rare/premium outerwear (Barbour, Belstaff, Grenfell, Gloverall) can hold price longer
- Seasonal items should be marked down faster when out of season
- Items bought for under £5 can be priced more aggressively for fast sale
