# Purpose
Generate simple reselling analytics from inventory data.

# Metrics
- average profit (sell price - buy price)
- ROI %
- days to sale
- best brands by ROI
- fastest-selling categories
- slowest-moving stock
- markdown effectiveness (did price drops lead to sales?)

# Workflow
1. Read current inventory and sold data from Google Sheets.
2. Calculate summary metrics.
3. Highlight top-performing and underperforming brands/categories.
4. Suggest pricing or sourcing adjustments.

# Style
Keep outputs concise, actionable, and numeric. No waffle.
